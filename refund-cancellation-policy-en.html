Refund and Cancellation Policy

: 2026

Overview

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations are handled for our services and courses.

48-Hour Refund Window

For eligible purchases, refund requests submitted within 48 hours are reviewed with priority.

Service Refund Eligibility

  • Request submitted within the allowed refund window
  • No substantial service delivery has been completed
  • Required booking and payment details are provided

Processing time: 5 to 10 business days after approval.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests submitted after the refund window
  • Work already delivered or approved by the client
  • Customized work already started based on client confirmation

Cancellation Process

  • Contact us by email with your order details
  • Our team reviews eligibility and confirms your request
  • You receive a response within 24 hours

Courses Policy

  • Course refunds are evaluated based on access and usage status
  • If course content is fully accessed, refund may not apply
  • Partial refunds may apply in verified cases

  • Include full order details for faster handling
  • Support responses are provided during business hours
